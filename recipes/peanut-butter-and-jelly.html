Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

This is a staple of the American household. The kids enjoy it, and secretly, it's your SO's favorite meal of yours.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons any flavor fruit jelly
  • 1 teaspoon peanut butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Spread jelly on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread.
  3. Spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side.
