Latte

Description

My take on our corner coffee shop's latte. I loved it so much I HAD to do something! Top with nutmeg or cinnamon if you like. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canned pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger brewed espresso

Steps

  1. Brew your espresso.
  2. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, whisk together the pumpkin, vanilla, sugar, pumpkin pie spice and milk.
  3. Warm over medium heat, whisking constantly, until hot and frothy. Do not bring to a boil.
  4. Pour the espresso into a mug and pour the pumpkin spiced milk over it.
